About me

I am a PhD student at the Center for Applied Mathematics, Tianjin University since September 2023, under the supervision of Professor Xun Yu.

My research interests lie in algebraic geometry. Recently, I worked on the automorphisms of hypersurfaces with Professors Song Yang and Xun Yu.
