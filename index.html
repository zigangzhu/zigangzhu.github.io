About me

I am a PhD student at the Center for Applied Mathematics, Tianjin University since September 2023, under the supervision of Professor Xun Yu.

My research interests lie in Algebraic Geometry. Recently, I have been working on the automorphisms of hypersurfaces with Professors Song Yang and Xun Yu.
